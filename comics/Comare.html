Comare

Comare is a story about lust, betrayal, desperation, and obsession set against the backdrop of old Hollywood. This fully colored twenty page comic book is the beginning of a six issue mini-series that harkens back to the dramatic romance comics that dominated sales alongside superhero books throughout the 1940's and 50's. A recent Kickstarter success!
