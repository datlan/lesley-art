Amazing World of Gumball

I've completed two 140+ page Amazing World of Gumball original graphic novels with Boom! Studios. They are a ton of fun to work on! I worked with artist Jenna Ayoub on AWOG vol 6: Midsummer Nightmare, which is out right now. I also worked on AWOG vol 7: The Storm, with artist Shadia Amin. The Storm hits the marketplace in August 2019!
