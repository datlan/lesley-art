High Crimes

High Crimes is a digital first comic by Christopher Sebela (writer, lettering) and Ibrahim Moustafa (art), published by Monkeybrain comics. The complete story is available in hardcover, published by Dark Horse Comics. Chris and Ibrahim brought me on as a color assistant for issues 7-12, as well as for their short story in the CBLDF Free Comic Book Day book. My job was to take the pages and color correct them to match the colors and tone of the previous issues, then render in the shadows. Ibrahim took it from there, tweaking what I'd done and adding all the cool bits of mood and texture and lighting to transform the page to that signature High Crimes look.
