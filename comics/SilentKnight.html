Silent Knight

Silent Knight is a four issue crime noir that I worked on with George O'Connor and DaFu Yu. Two successful kickstarters brought the book to life! Silent Knight imagines Santa Claus as a crime-fighting private eye who uses his Santa-powers to help find lost children. If you like your holiday stories with a twist—and lots of headbutting—this is the book for you.
