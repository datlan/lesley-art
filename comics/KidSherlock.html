Kid Sherlock

Kid Sherlock is a fun new series from Action Lab Entertainment. I've had a great time working on it with writer Justin Phillips and artist Sean Gregory Miller. We've got four issues in the bag so far - check your FLCS for single issues. They'll be collected in a single volume in November! Pre-orders available from your aforementioned FLCS, as well as Amazon, Target, and Powell's!
